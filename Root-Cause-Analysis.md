# Root Cause Analysis (5 Whys)

## Problem: Delayed and unclear scholarship communication

- Why are students not getting clear updates?
  - Information is shared verbally or informally

- Why is information informal?
  - No standard communication process exists

- Why is there no standard process?
  - No single owner for scholarship communication

- Why is ownership unclear?
  - Roles and responsibilities are not clearly defined

- Root Causes Identified:
  - No single communication channel
  - No tracking of student queries
  - Unclear roles between office and academic staff
